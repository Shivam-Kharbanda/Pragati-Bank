About Us

Welcome to Pragati Bank, where your financial growth is our priority. Established in 2000, Pragati Bank has been dedicated to providing reliable and efficient banking services to our valued customers. Our mission is to empower individuals and businesses by offering innovative financial solutions and exceptional customer service.

Our Vision

At Pragati Bank, we envision a world where everyone has access to financial resources that help them achieve their dreams. We strive to be the most trusted banking partner, known for our commitment to customer satisfaction, integrity, and community development.

Our Mission

  • To provide comprehensive financial services that meet the diverse needs of our customers.
  • To foster a culture of innovation, continuous improvement, and excellence.
  • To contribute to the economic development of our community through responsible banking practices.

Our Values

We believe in:

  • Integrity: Upholding the highest standards of honesty and transparency in all our dealings.
  • Customer Focus: Placing our customers at the heart of everything we do.
  • Innovation: Continuously seeking new and better ways to serve our customers.
  • Community: Making a positive impact on the communities we serve.
